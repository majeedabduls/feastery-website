About Feastery

We believe food should be simple, natural, and delicious. No chemicals, no shortcuts — only purity.

Our Story

Feastery was born with a simple mission: bring back the authentic taste of Kerala snacks using natural ingredients and traditional recipes.

From banana chips fried in pure coconut oil to handcrafted jackfruit fries, every Feastery product is made with love, purity, and tradition.
